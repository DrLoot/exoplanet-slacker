FAQs

Quick answers to common questions from Slack.

How do I join the NASA Exoplanet Watch Slack?

Join the Exoplanet Watch Slack community -> Exoplanet Watch Slack

Where do I post my first light curve?

Share your light curves in the Slack #data-upload-aavso channel and tag your target; include any other data you'd like to share.

What is the easiest way to get help with EXOTIC?

Ask in Slack #exotic channel with as much info as possible; the issue, screenshots, logs, etc. A community member will jump in and help as soon as they can.
